The Process

How it works

Three steps between your heart and the words of Allah.

01

Write freely

No structure, no format. Write however you feel — in English, in Urdu, in fragments. The app understands emotion, not grammar.

02

Emotion is detected

Your words are analysed for emotional state — across 14 emotions including grief, anxiety, gratitude, loneliness, and hope.

03

The Quran responds

Semantically matched Ayahs are surfaced — not by keyword, but by meaning. The verse that speaks to your exact state, right now.

04

Track your journey

See how your emotional state shifts over time. Bookmark verses that moved you. Return to your history whenever you need.
